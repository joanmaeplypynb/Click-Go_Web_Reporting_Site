Additional Findings

Extra insights gathered during usability testing and system evaluation.

SUMMARY OF ADDITIONAL FINDINGS

Based on the responses gathered from the participants, several additional findings were observed during the usability testing of the Click&Go system. Most respondents gave high ratings in statements related to ease of use, confidence, and willingness to use the system frequently, which suggests that the system was generally accepted positively by users.


High User Acceptance

Most participants agreed that they would like to use the system frequently, showing positive acceptance and user interest in the platform.

Easy to Learn

Respondents found the system easy to use and believed that most people could learn to use it quickly without difficulty.

Strong User Confidence

Many users reported feeling confident while using the system, indicating that the interface provided enough clarity and guidance.

Reduced Need for Technical Support

Most participants did not strongly feel the need for technical assistance, suggesting that the system was understandable even for first-time users.

Minor Complexity Issues

Although the general feedback was positive, some users still experienced minor complexity and inconsistency in certain parts of the system.

Need for Better Error Prevention

Some respondents suggested clearer confirmation prompts and more visible instructions to help avoid mistakes during booking and transactions.



Conclusion: The findings show that Click&Go performed well in usability, particularly in simplicity, learnability, and user confidence. However, improving consistency, reducing minor complexity, and strengthening confirmation features can further enhance the overall user experience.
