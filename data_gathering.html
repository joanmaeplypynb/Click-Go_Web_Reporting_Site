Participant Background


The study involved 20 participants from the Bachelor of Arts in Communication (BACOM) 3rd year program. These respondents were selected to evaluate the usability of the Click&Go system. A Google Meet session was conducted to introduce the system, explain its features, and guide the participants on how to accomplish the usability testing survey. After the walkthrough, the participants interacted with the system and provided their feedback through the survey. The data gathering process was documented through video recordings and screenshots.
