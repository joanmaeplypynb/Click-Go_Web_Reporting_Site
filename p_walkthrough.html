Prototype Walkthrough

The Prototype Walkthrough presents the complete user journey of the Click&Go system, showing how users interact with the application from the welcome screen up to the final booking confirmation. Each screen was designed to provide a simple, smooth, and user-friendly experience for commuters. The user flow starts from the Welcome Screen, followed by the Home Page where users begin the booking process. They proceed to the Booking Page to enter travel details, review all information on the Confirmation Page, and finally receive the Success Page after completing the transaction.
